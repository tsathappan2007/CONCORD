## 📌 Overview

CONCORD is an AI-powered autonomous contract negotiation platform that enables two parties to negotiate contract terms through intelligent AI agents while preserving each party's confidential negotiation constraints.

Instead of lengthy email chains and manual bargaining, CONCORD allows AI agents to negotiate on behalf of users within predefined boundaries and generate a mutually acceptable agreement for final human approval.

Our goal is simple:

> **Reduce negotiation time from days to minutes while ensuring transparency, privacy, and trust.**

---

# 🚀 Problem Statement

Contract negotiation is one of the slowest phases of any agreement.

Whether it is

- Freelancer ↔ Client
- Employer ↔ Employee
- Vendor ↔ Business
- Tenant ↔ Landlord

the process usually involves

- Endless back-and-forth emails
- Manual revisions
- Delayed decision making
- Expensive legal consultation
- Emotion-driven negotiations

Small businesses and individuals often cannot afford professional negotiators or legal teams.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

CONCORD introduces **AI-mediated autonomous negotiation**.

Each participant is assigned a dedicated AI negotiation agent.

Users privately configure:

- Preferred values
- Walk-away limits
- Priorities

The AI agents negotiate autonomously while keeping confidential constraints hidden from the opposing party.

Only the final agreement is presented for human approval.

# 🔐 Private Constraint Negotiation

Each party specifies

- Preferred value
- Walk-away range
- Priority

Example

| Parameter | Party A | Party B |
|------------|----------|----------|
| Hourly Rate | $100 | $140 |
| Delivery | 8 Weeks | 6 Weeks |
| Payment | 15 Days | 7 Days |

These constraints remain **private**.

The AI only exchanges proposals — never confidential limits.

# 🤖 AI Architecture

CONCORD is powered by a **Qwen-based negotiation engine** enhanced through **LoRA (Low-Rank Adaptation)** for domain-specific contract negotiation.

### AI Pipeline

```text
Contract

↓

Document Parsing

↓

Constraint Understanding

↓

Qwen Negotiation Agent

↓

LoRA Fine-Tuned Negotiation Reasoning

↓

Proposal Generation

↓

Counter Proposal

↓

Agreement
```

### Why Qwen?

- Open-source
- Strong reasoning capability
- Excellent instruction following
- Enterprise deployable
- Cost-efficient
- Supports domain-specific fine-tuning

### Why LoRA?

Instead of retraining billions of parameters,

LoRA fine-tunes only lightweight adapter layers.

Benefits include

- Faster training
- Lower GPU requirements
- Reduced deployment cost
- Better specialization for negotiation tasks

# 🔒 Privacy

Each user's negotiation constraints are encrypted and accessible only to their AI negotiation agent.

Neither party can view

- Preferred values
- Walk-away limits
- Priorities

Only negotiated proposals are exchanged.
